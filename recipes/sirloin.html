Sirloin Steak with Garlic Butter

Description

A recipe for when I want to feel like I'm having luxury. Taken from https://www.allrecipes.com/recipe/14554/sirloin-steak-with-garlic-butter/.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup butter
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 4 pounds beef top sirloin steaks
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil the grate.

  2. Melt butter in a small saucepan over medium-low heat.

  3. Stir in minced garlic and garlic powder. Set aside.

  4. Season both sides of each steak with salt and pepper.

  5. Transfer steaks to warmed plates; brush the tops liberally with garlic butter and allow to rest for 2 to 3 minutes before serving.
